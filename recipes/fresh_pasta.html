Fresh Pasta

Description

Eggs and 00 flour are hand mixed until they come together. When it begins to take shape, the dough is kneaded and adjusted until it forms
a dense, silky dough with the appropximate texture of an earlobe. After a brief rest, the dough is shaped according to desire and boiled for
no more than 3 minutes in salty water.

Time Investment

2 hours: 15 mixing, 30 minute rest, 1 hour shaping and cooking

Ingredients

  • One fork, a mixing bowl, and a cutting board
  • 400g 00 flour, more for kneading
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 pinches of salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Whisk salt and flour together, then place in a heap on counter
  3. Using two fingers, make a well in the center of the flour heap
  4. Crack the eggs into the well
  5. Break the yolks with a fork and begin scrambling them in the well
  6. Begin pulling flour from the sides into the mixing of the egg
  7. Once the flour and egg become too hard to mix, clean the fork and set it aside
  8. With clean hands, pull flour over the mixture and begin to push the mixture back and forth,
    keeping flour between your hands and the yolk
  9. Once it comes together, knead for 10 minutes, then cover and let rest 30 minutes
  10. Roll it out, cut it into sections, and shape as desired
